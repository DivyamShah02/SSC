Process Flow:

Form Submission:
- Once the client submits the form with their preferences.

First-Level Check:
- The initial screening will be based on the following criteria:
    - Area
    - Number of bedrooms
    - Carpet area
    - Budget
    - Type of apartment

- Only properties that meet these conditions will be selected for further evaluation.

Scoring Logic:
- A scoring logic will then be applied to the remaining filters to rank the properties.
